October 16

9:15—Style Sheets Demo

Assignment

Begin work on Project 4. Collect your images. Bring to class a map of your book, showing considerations for signals, pacing, page counts. Set up your InDesign document with Object, Paragraph, and Character Styles. Roughly lay out the ‘middle’, showing options. 1:1 chats.

Journal

Write a post-mortem about your first experience with using a grid to aid in the flow of information. Some prompts: What did you find hard about it, and conversely, what was liberating? What did you find restrictive and superfluous, in your opinion? What ideas did a grid unlock for you that you hadn’t thought of before? What did you think was just ‘design-world hocus pocus’, if at all? What was something you would do with your newsletter, given more time? What’s the difference between a ‘grid’ in something like a book v.s the system laid out for a newspaper or a magazine, on print or web or mobile? Due 10/23.
