Assignment

For Thursday, think about your assigned small business and do some preliminary research. Bring at least 10 wordmark sketches to class, using at least three different typefaces from the list.


Journal

Journal entries are due on a weekly basis. For Monday, write 50 to 100 words about the typeface you have chosen for your project. Include a few notes about its history, as well as initial personal thoughts. Include images of the typeface in use, if applicable. The goal is for you to be able to look back by the end of the semester to see how your understanding of typefaces deepen over time.
